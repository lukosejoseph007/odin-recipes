Description

Enjoy a delightful and simple breakfast with our scrumptious Bread Omelette! This quick recipe combines the goodness of eggs with the comforting taste of bread, enhanced with a burst of flavors from onions, green chillies, and curry leaves.

Ready in just a few minutes, it's perfect for busy mornings or anytime you crave a delicious and nutritious meal.

Ingredients

  • Bread - 2 slices
  • Butter - 3 teaspoons
  • Egg - 2 nos
  • Water - 1 tablespoon(15ml)
  • Green chillies - 1 to 2 nos (chopped)
  • Crushed black pepper - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Chopped onions - 1/4 cups
  • Curry leaves - 1 sprig (chopped)
  • Salt - 1 pinch

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, crack the eggs and whisk them well with a tablespoon of water, crushed black pepper, and a pinch of salt. Set aside.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add 2 teaspoons of butter, letting it melt and coat the pan.
  3. Add the chopped onions, green chillies, and curry leaves to the pan. Saut√© until the onions turn translucent and the mixture becomes aromatic.
  4. Pour the whisked egg mixture onto the pan and spread it evenly.
  5. Place the 2 slices of bread on top of the omelette in the pan, gently pressing them down so they stick to the eggs.
  6. Allow the omelette to cook for a couple of minutes until the bottom side is golden and the eggs are mostly set.
  7. Now, flip the bread omelette to cook the other side. Add the remaining teaspoon of butter around the edges of the omelette.
  8. Cook until the eggs are fully set and the bread is toasted to your desired level of crispiness.
  9. Carefully transfer the bread omelette to a plate and serve hot. You can enjoy it as is or with your favorite sauce or condiments.
  10. Enjoy your delectable Bread Omelette, a delightful fusion of flavors and textures that will brighten up your day!

Note: Feel free to customize this recipe by adding other vegetables, cheese, or any ingredients of your choice.

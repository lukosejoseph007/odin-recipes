Description

This Spicy Soya Chunks Stir-Fry is a quick and easy recipe packed with delicious flavors. It combines protein-rich soya chunks with aromatic spices, creating a mouthwatering dish that can be served as a satisfying main or a delectable side.

Ingredients

  • Water - 1 litre
  • Salt - 1.5 teaspoon
  • Soya chunks - 100 gm
  • Cooking oil - 3 tablespoons
  • Ginger - 1 inch piece
  • Garlic - 6 cloves
  • Green chillies - 2 nos
  • Onion - 2 nos (200gm)
  • Curry leaves - 3 sprigs
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Kashmiri chilli powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Garam masala - 1 teaspoon
  • Water - 1/4 cup
  • Lime / Lemon juice - 1 teaspoon
  • Tomato ketchup - 1 tablespoon
  • Crushed pepper - 1/2 teaspoon

Instructions

  1. Boil 1 litre of water with 1.5 teaspoons of salt in a saucepan. Add the soya chunks to the boiling water and let them cook for 8-10 minutes or until they become soft and plump. Drain the water and rinse the soya chunks under cold water. Squeeze out any excess water and set them aside.
  2. In a large skillet or pan, heat 3 tablespoons of cooking oil over medium heat. Add finely chopped ginger, minced garlic, and sliced green chillies. Saute for a minute until they release their aroma.
  3. Add thinly sliced onions and curry leaves to the pan. Cook until the onions turn golden brown and caramelized.
  4. Now, add 1/2 teaspoon of salt, 1 tablespoon of coriander powder, 1 tablespoon of Kashmiri chilli powder, and 1/4 teaspoon of turmeric powder to the pan. Mix the spices with the onions and cook for 1-2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  5. Toss in the boiled soya chunks and stir-fry them with the onion and spice mixture for 3-4 minutes, ensuring the chunks are evenly coated with the spices.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of water into the pan to create a thick gravy and let it simmer for a few more minutes until the soya chunks absorb the flavors.
  7. Finally, add 1 teaspoon of lime or lemon juice, 1 tablespoon of tomato ketchup, and 1/2 teaspoon of crushed pepper. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  8. Sprinkle 1 teaspoon of garam masala over the stir-fry and give it a final toss.
  9. Your Spicy Soya Chunks Stir-Fry is ready to serve. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired and enjoy it with rice, roti, or as a standalone snack!

This recipe serves approximately 3-4 people. Adjust the spice levels according to your taste preference. Enjoy your delicious and nutritious soya chunks creation!

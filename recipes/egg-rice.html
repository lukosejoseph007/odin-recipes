Description

Egg rice is a delightful and flavorful dish that combines fluffy cooked rice with scrambled eggs and a medley of aromatic spices and vegetables.

This easy-to-make recipe is a perfect balance of textures and tastes, making it an ideal option for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice - 8 cups
  • Egg - 5 nos
  • Cooking oil - 4 tablespoons
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Ginger - 1/2 inch piece (chopped)
  • Garlic - 8 cloves (chopped)
  • Green Chillies - 1 no (chopped)
  • Sliced Onion - 2 nos (250gms)
  • Carrot - 1/2 cup (chopped)
  • Green Beans - 1/2 cup (chopped)
  • Salt - 3/4 + 1.25 teaspoon
  • Kashmiri Chilli Powder - 1.5 teaspoons
  • Coriander Powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric Powder - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Garam Masala - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Water - 1/4 cup (60ml)
  • Lime / Lemon Juice - 1/2 tablespoon
  • Black Pepper Powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Coriander leaves - 1/2 cup

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet or pan, heat the cooking oil over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Stir in the chopped ginger, garlic, and green chillies. Sauté until the raw aroma disappears.
  3. Add the sliced onions and cook until they turn golden brown.
  4. Toss in the chopped carrots and green beans. Sauté for a few minutes until they are tender yet crisp.
  5. Sprinkle 3/4 teaspoon of salt, Kashmiri chilli powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Mix well to coat the vegetables evenly.
  6. Lower the heat and add the cooked rice to the pan. Gently toss the rice with the spiced vegetables until well combined.
  7. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs with 1.25 teaspoons of salt. Push the rice mixture to one side of the pan and pour the beaten eggs into the empty space.
  8. Scramble the eggs until they are cooked through, breaking them into smaller pieces as they cook.
  9. Now, mix the scrambled eggs with the rice and vegetables. Add 1/4 cup of water to keep the rice moist and prevent sticking.
  10. Drizzle lime or lemon juice over the egg rice, and sprinkle black pepper powder for an extra kick of flavor.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves, and your delicious egg rice is ready to be served hot.

Enjoy the delectable flavors of this simple yet satisfying egg dish!

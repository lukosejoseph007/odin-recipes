Chicken Curry Recipe

Description

Indulge in the aromatic flavors of this mouthwatering chicken curry! Tender pieces of chicken, sautéed with onions, green chilies, and a blend of aromatic spices, make this dish an absolute delight.

The combination of ginger-garlic paste, coriander powder, and chicken masala creates a rich and flavorful base, while tomatoes add a hint of tanginess. Garnished with curry leaves and a sprinkle of black pepper, this hearty curry is best enjoyed with steaming rice or fluffy naan.

Ingredients

  • Chicken - 1200gms (cleaned)
  • Cooking oil - 4 tablespoons
  • Onion - 4 nos
  • Green Chillies - 2 nos
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder - 2 tablespoons
  • Chilli powder - 2/3 tablespoon
  • Chicken masala - 1 tablespoon
  • Tomato - 1 no
  • Water - 1.5 cup (360ml)
  • Curry leaves - 2 sprigs
  • Black pepper powder - 1/2 teaspoon

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot or skillet over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until they become translucent.
  2. Add green chilies and ginger-garlic paste to the onions. Sauté for another 1-2 minutes until the raw aroma of ginger-garlic disappears.
  3. Now, add the cleaned chicken pieces to the pot and mix well with the onion mixture. Cook the chicken for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it starts to brown slightly.
  4. Add salt, turmeric powder, coriander powder, chili powder, and chicken masala to the chicken. Stir everything together, ensuring the spices coat the chicken evenly.
  5. Next, add diced tomatoes and give it a quick mix. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the tomatoes become soft.
  6. Pour in 1.5 cups of water and bring the mixture to a boil. Once it starts boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the curry simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  7. While the curry simmers, add the curry leaves for an authentic Kerala touch.
  8. Once the chicken is cooked, check the seasoning and adjust salt and spices according to your taste.
  9. Finally, sprinkle black pepper powder over the curry, giving it a mild spicy kick.

Your quick and easy Kerala-style chicken curry is now ready to be served. Enjoy this delightful dish with steamed rice, appam, or Kerala parotta for an authentic South Indian dining experience!
